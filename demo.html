Who We Are

The Ashok Singh Educational Foundation (ASEF) is a non-profit educational trust committed to the upliftment of society through the power of education. Founded with the core belief that knowledge is the key to breaking the cycle of poverty, we strive to provide opportunities to underprivileged students.

Based in the spiritual city of Deoghar, Jharkhand, our work focuses on bridging educational gaps and fostering an environment where learning is accessible to all.

Our Programs & Services

Comprehensive initiatives designed for growth.

Merit Scholarships

Financial aid provided to meritorious students from economically weaker sections to help them continue their higher education without financial burden.

Library & Resources

We establish and maintain small libraries in rural areas, providing textbooks, reference materials, and storybooks to encourage reading habits.

Vocational Training

Skill development workshops for youth and women to enhance employability, including computer basics, handicrafts, and agricultural techniques.

Community Aid

Distribution of school uniforms, bags, and stationery to remote village schools to ensure students have the basic tools for learning.
