About Our Foundation

Dedicated to creating brighter futures through education in Deoghar, Jharkhand

Our Foundation Story

The Ashok Singh Educational Foundation was established in Deoghar, Jharkhand with a simple yet powerful vision: to ensure that every child, regardless of their economic background, has access to quality education and the opportunity to build a better future.

Founded by brothers Ashok Kumar Singh and Krishna Kumar Singh, our journey began with the observation that many bright children in rural areas were dropping out of school due to financial constraints. What started as a small initiative to support a few children with study materials has grown into a comprehensive educational support program reaching hundreds of children across multiple villages.

We believe that education is not just about academic learning, but about empowering children with the knowledge, skills, and confidence they need to break the cycle of poverty and become contributing members of society.

Our Vision & Mission

Guiding principles that drive everything we do

Our Vision

A society where every child has access to quality education and the opportunity to reach their full potential, regardless of their economic background.

Our Mission

To identify, support, and nurture underprivileged children by providing complete study materials, educational guidance, and mentoring to help them succeed academically and personally.
