Our Programs

Structured educational initiatives for comprehensive support

Comprehensive Educational Programs

Designed to address different aspects of educational support for underprivileged children

Our programs are designed to provide holistic educational support to underprivileged children in Deoghar, Jharkhand. Each program addresses specific needs while working together to create comprehensive educational development.

From basic material support to advanced academic guidance, our programs ensure that children receive the right support at the right time in their educational journey.

All programs are implemented with regular monitoring and evaluation to ensure maximum impact and effectiveness.

Child Education Sponsorship Program

Transform a Child's Life Through Education

Our Child Education Sponsorship Program allows individuals and organizations to sponsor a specific child's complete education. Sponsors provide financial support for school fees, uniforms, textbooks, and other educational expenses.

Sponsors receive regular updates about their sponsored child's academic progress, including report cards, photographs, and personal letters. This creates a meaningful connection between the sponsor and the child.

What Sponsorship Includes:
  • Annual school fees
  • Complete set of textbooks
  • School uniform (2 sets)
  • Shoes and socks
  • School bag and stationery
  • After-school coaching support

Free Books & Uniforms Program

Ensuring Access to Essential Educational Materials

Every year, we distribute free textbooks, notebooks, school uniforms, and educational kits to underprivileged students across Deoghar district. This program ensures that financial constraints don't prevent children from attending school with dignity.

The distribution drives are conducted at the beginning of each academic year, with additional mid-year support for students who need replacement materials.

Distribution Includes:
  • Grade-appropriate textbooks
  • Notebooks for all subjects
  • School uniform (shirt, pant/skirt)
  • School shoes and socks
  • School bag
  • Geometry box and stationery

Coaching & Tutoring Program

Academic Excellence Through Supplementary Education

Our Coaching & Tutoring Program provides free after-school academic support to students who are struggling with their studies. Qualified teachers and volunteers conduct regular coaching classes in core subjects.

The program focuses on improving fundamental concepts, examination preparation, and developing good study habits. Special attention is given to students from government schools who often lack access to additional academic support.

Program Features:
  • Daily after-school classes
  • Focus on Mathematics, Science, English
  • Small group and individual attention
  • Regular assessments and feedback
  • Exam preparation support
  • Study skills development

Digital Learning Program

Bridging the Digital Divide in Education

Our Digital Learning Program introduces underprivileged children to technology and digital learning tools. In today's world, digital literacy is as important as traditional literacy, and this program ensures that rural children are not left behind.

We conduct basic computer literacy classes, introduce educational software and apps, and teach internet safety. The program also includes occasional exposure to coding and digital creativity tools.

Program Components:
  • Basic computer operation
  • Educational software training
  • Internet safety education
  • Digital research skills
  • Introduction to coding
  • Creative digital tools
