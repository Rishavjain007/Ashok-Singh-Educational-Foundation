Support Our Mission

Your donation can change a child's life through education

Invest in a Child's Future

Every donation to Ashok Singh Educational Foundation goes directly toward providing educational support to underprivileged children in Deoghar, Jharkhand. Your contribution helps us:

  • Provide textbooks, notebooks, and stationery
  • Support school fees and uniform costs
  • Offer after-school coaching and tutoring
  • Conduct digital literacy programs
  • Enable school enrollment for out-of-school children

100% of your donation is utilized for educational purposes. We maintain complete transparency and provide regular updates on how your contribution is making a difference.

Your donation is eligible for tax exemption
